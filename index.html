ABOUT

Digimark Int’l

We are a full-service digital marketing agency expert in digital marketing strategy, website design & development, social media marketing, and viral and search marketing. Different than other agencies working project-specific, Digimark Int’l puts sustainability at the centre of online communication.

We are a collection of digital strategists, creative managers, account managers, designers, and engineers…who have perfectionism in our hearts to satisfy our clients.

We understand that building a strong relationship between a brand and its customers needs a long time, and that’s why we prefer to work continuously with a brand rather than just focusing on short-term projects. We act as both an implementer and their brand’s consultant, giving them great advice and creative ideas for marketing communication in a digital environment.

As a result, the best benefit Mix Digital brings to the brand is: “Sustainable interaction”.

With an in-house creative team that possesses a wide range of creative talents and disciplines, we can bring your thoughts to life and assist boost your business.
